Must-Do Activities in India

🕌 Spiritual Journey

Visit sacred temples like Varanasi, Golden Temple, or Kedarnath for a soulful experience.

🏔️ Himalayan Trekking

Explore breathtaking treks in Himachal, Uttarakhand, and Kashmir valleys.

🎭 Cultural Festivals

Experience Holi, Diwali, Navratri, and local fairs like Pushkar Mela and Hornbill Festival.

🌊 Beach Adventures

Enjoy surfing, parasailing, or sunset yoga on the beaches of Goa, Kerala & Andaman.

🍛 Food Exploration

Try spicy street food in Delhi, Dosas in South India, and rich biryanis in Hyderabad.

🐘 Wildlife Safari

Go on thrilling safaris in Ranthambore, Jim Corbett, or Kaziranga National Parks.
